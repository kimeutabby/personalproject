About Nairobi Hospital

Nairobi Hospital is a trusted private healthcare provider established in 1954. We are dedicated to delivering excellent medical care with compassion, advanced technology, and a team of skilled professionals.

Our Mission

We provide high-quality healthcare services using modern facilities and experienced medical staff. Our mission is to support the health and wellbeing of the communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be the leading hospital in East Africa for patient care, medical innovation, and compassionate support.

Experienced Doctors

Specialists in cardiology, surgery, pediatrics, neurology, and more.

Modern Facilities

Equipped operating rooms, imaging labs, and emergency care centers.

Trusted Care

Patient-focused service that emphasizes safety, comfort, and recovery.

Community Support

Health education, outreach programs, and community wellness initiatives.

Our History

Since opening in 1954, Nairobi Hospital has grown into one of the most respected healthcare institutions in Kenya. We continue to expand our services while remaining committed to compassionate medical care.
